The world famous Bohuslän Äggost!

About the dish

Äggost is a traditional desert for feasts in the region of Bohuslän, Sweden. Directly translated into "Egg-cheese" Äggost is a mild, sweet tart best served with blackberries.

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Cream
  • Eggs
  • Sourcream

How to make Äggost

  • Mix the milk and cream in a saucecpan. Heat untill the boiling point
  • Mix the sourcream and eggs into a bucket and use a whisk. Then add to the milk and cream while stiring.
  • Stir while the batch simmers, wait untill it starts to separate (3-5min). Remove the saucepan from the stove and cool it for 15 minutes.
  • Wrap curd and sugar in egg cheese form or colander. Let drain in the fridge overnight.
  • Turn upside down on a platter and garnish with blackberries.
